
## Features & Targets — Dataset Preparation (Week 4)

### Target Variables
- recommended_material (classification)
- sustainability_score (regression, 0–100)
- cost_efficiency_category (Low / Medium / High)

### Feature Groups
Material Properties:
- Material Type, Density, Strength Score, Moisture_resistance, Thermal_resistance, Load_handling, CO2_per_kg, Biodegradation Time (days), Recyclability Category, Renewable_Content_Percentage

Product Requirements:
- Product Category, Fragility Score, Weight Category, Moisture Sensitivity, Temperature Sensitivity, Transportation Distance, Expected Shelf Life (days)

Cost & Operations:
- Cost_per_kg, Manufacturing Cost, Supply Chain Availability, Regional Restrictions
